# Revenue Intelligence Case Studies

Welcome to a collection of real-world revenue intelligence case studies. As a **Statistical Business Architect**, I design systems that bridge the gap between raw daily operations and high-level data-driven decisions.

This repository documents the transformation of business problems into measurable statistical frameworks. These aren't just tutorials; they are live deployments solving actual friction points in revenue tracking and operational efficiency.

## Philosophy
Adoption over sophistication. A complex system that nobody uses is a failed system. My approach focuses on:
1. **Low Friction:** Meeting the users where they already are (e.g., mobile-first Google Sheets).
2. **Data Integrity:** Building statistical guardrails to ensure accuracy at the point of entry.
3. **Actionable Intelligence:** Turning raw numbers into automated restock alerts and revenue dashboards.
