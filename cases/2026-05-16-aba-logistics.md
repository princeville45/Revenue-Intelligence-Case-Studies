# Case Study: Reducing Lead-Time Variability in Aba Market Distribution

## Background
Distribution to Aba's informal markets suffered from 3-day lead time variance.

## The Problem
Unpredictable transit times led to stockouts for high-velocity wholesalers.

## The Strategy
Implemented a decentralized distribution point (DDP) strategy and synchronized order cycles with local transport peak hours.

## Impact
Lead time variance reduced from 72 hours to 12 hours. Fulfillment rates increased from 82% to 96%.
