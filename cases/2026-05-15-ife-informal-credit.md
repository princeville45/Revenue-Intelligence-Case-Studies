# Case Study: Managing Credit Risk in Ife's Informal Wholesale Market

## Context
Wholesale distributors in Osun State often extend credit to retailers without formal collateral.

## Problem
High default rates (12%+) during seasonal downturns due to lack of visibility into retailer cash flow.

## Solution
Implemented a peer-validated trust scoring system and a rolling credit limit based on 30-day purchase velocity.

## Results
Default rates dropped to 4.5% within two quarters, while retailer loyalty increased by 20%.
