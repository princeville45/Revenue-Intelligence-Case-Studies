# Case Study: Optimizing Last-Mile FMCG Distribution in Lagos

## Context
Lagos traffic congestion increases delivery lead times by 40% on average.

## The Problem
Static routing led to missed delivery windows and fuel inefficiency.

## The Data-Driven Solution
Implemented a hub-and-spoke model using micro-fulfillment centers in Ikeja and Surulere, combined with real-time congestion weighting.

## Impact
Reduced average delivery time from 6 hours to 3.5 hours and decreased fuel costs by 18%.
