# Case Study: Mitigating Transit Risks on the Lagos-Ibadan Expressway

## Context
The Lagos-Ibadan Expressway is the busiest commercial artery in Nigeria. High transit variability (2-8 hours) creates massive disruptions in wholesale restocking.

## Data-Driven Solution
Implemented a dynamic departure window based on historical Google Maps API data. Peak traffic analysis suggested 4:00 AM departures reduced 'Time-in-Transit' by 45% compared to 8:00 AM.

## Outcome
Average lead time reduced from 5.2 days to 3.8 days. Vehicle maintenance costs (overheating/idling) dropped by 12%.
