# Case Study: Mitigating Margin Erosion during NGN Devaluation

## Context
Rapid Naira devaluation in 2024-2025 created a 20% margin gap for imported FMCG goods.

## Strategy
Transitioned from monthly to weekly price indexing based on parallel market volatility and implemented 'Forward-Pricing' for inventory restocking cycles.

## Outcome
Gross margin stabilized within 14 days of implementation. Market share in Lagos and Onitsha remained steady despite price hikes.
