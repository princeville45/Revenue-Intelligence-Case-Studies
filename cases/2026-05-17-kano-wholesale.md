# Case Study: Scaling Wholesale Distribution in Kano via Credit Normalization

## Overview
Wholesalers in Kano's Sabon Gari market faced scaling limits due to manual credit assessments.

## Problem
High manual processing time (48h) for retailer credit requests hindered sales velocity during peak harvest seasons.

## Solution
Introduced a statistical credit scoring model using historical purchase frequency and seasonal yield forecasts.

## Results
Credit approval time dropped to 2 hours. Order volume increased by 35% in the first quarter of implementation.
