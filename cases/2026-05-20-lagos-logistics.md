# Case Study: Optimizing Last-Mile Logistics in Lagos Traffic

## Challenge
Lagos traffic congestion increases delivery times by 40%, affecting customer satisfaction and increasing operational costs for wholesale distributors.

## Solution
Deployed a statistical routing model that prioritizes 'early-bird' deliveries and uses motorbike-based relay hubs in high-density areas (Ikeja, Yaba).

## Results
Delivery completion rate improved by 25%. Fuel and maintenance costs decreased by 15%. Net Promoter Score (NPS) rose by 12 points within six months.
