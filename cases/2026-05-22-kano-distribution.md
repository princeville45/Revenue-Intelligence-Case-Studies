# Case Study: Optimizing Grain Distribution in Kano (Northern Nigeria)

## Problem
High seasonal price volatility in Kano grain markets (Dawanau) caused by poor storage and inefficient transport timing.

## Solution
Integrated satellite-based crop yield forecasting with a decentralized warehouse system to buffer supply during lean months.

## Impact
Average price stability improved by 18%. Post-harvest losses decreased by 22% through better climate-controlled transit routing.
