# Case Study: Optimizing Supply Chain Resilience in Onitsha Main Market

## Problem
Fragmented logistics and high cash-reliance in Onitsha Main Market led to 15% stockout rates during peak regional demand.

## Strategy
Implemented a 'hub-and-spoke' distribution model and a digital inventory management system integrated with local mobile money agents.

## Impact
Stockout rates dropped to 4%. Working capital efficiency improved by 22% due to faster inventory turnover.
